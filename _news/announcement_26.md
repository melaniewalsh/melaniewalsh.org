Two of my projects won [**Digital Humanities Awards 2025**](http://dhawards.org/dhawards2025/results/): ["Bears Will Be Boys"](https://pudding.cool/2025/07/kids-books/) (with Russell Samora, Michelle Pera-McGhee, and Jan Diehm) won **Best Digital Humanities Short Publication**, and [Responsible Datasets in Context](https://www.responsible-datasets-in-context.com/) won **Best Digital Humanities Training Material**.
